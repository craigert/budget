Privacy & architecture

Your data stays with you

  • 🔒100% local storage — all data lives in your browser's IndexedDB via Dexie.js. Nothing is transmitted.
  • 🚫No account required — no sign-up, no email, no password.
  • 📤Export any time — download your transactions as CSV whenever you want.
  • 🧩Open codebase — built with SvelteKit, fully inspectable and self-hostable.
